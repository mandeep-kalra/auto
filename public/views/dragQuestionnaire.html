Instructions for taking this exercise

You have to rank a set of 18 adjectives that describe you AS YOU ARE. Detailed instructions are given below.
  1. The adjectives you see stacked in a column below can be moved UP or DOWN by using the mouse and dragging them - one adjective at a time.
  2. Choose the adjective that in your view best describes you AS YOU ARE and stack it - right on top of the list.
  3. Choose another adjective that describes you next best, and move it below the one placed right on top.
  4. Repeat this process till you have arranged all 18 adjectives in sequence, the word on top of the list being closest to describing you as you see yourself, and the word at the bottom describing you the least in relation to the other words.
  5. After completion, press the submit button at the bottom.
